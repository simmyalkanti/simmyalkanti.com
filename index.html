Beyond The Listing

87% of agents fail their first year. In my first year, I closed 6 transactions.

The most common misconception about realtors is that because it is not a 9-5, it must be an easy job. The only accuracy in that statement is that it is not a 9-5; it is a lifestyle. My mentors taught me early that real estate is not just a job. It is who you are around the clock.

For my sellers, that means relentless exposure and constant prospecting. For my buyers, that means networking day and night to find the best on and off market opportunities.

I was born and raised in Middlesex County, built deep roots in Bergen and Monmouth through family, and now call Hudson County home. These are not just markets to me. They are my communities.

The first home I ever sold was my mother's. I brought everything I had to that transaction, and I bring that same level of care to every client who trusts me with one of the biggest decisions of their life.
